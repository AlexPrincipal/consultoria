## Core Features:

- Homepage Design: Create a visually striking homepage in line with corporate design principles, featuring a hero section, service overview, and credibility elements.
- Sticky Navigation Bar: Implement a fixed navigation bar providing constant access to key site areas, like Services, About Us, FAQ, and a prominent 'Schedule a Consultation' call-to-action button.
- Service Overview Grid: Present key practice areas, such as consulting, compliance, and representation, via an engaging grid layout with clear, concise descriptions and direct pathways to deeper information.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Royal Blue (#4169E1) for buttons, underlines, and primary icons to convey trust and authority.
- Background color: Deep Charcoal (#121212) to provide a modern and sophisticated feel.
- Accent color: Soft gray (#333333) in the Testimonials section, serving as a neutral area and gently highlighting content. 
- Body font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) provides a modern, objective, and neutral aesthetic.
- Headline font: 'PT Sans' (sans-serif) is used for headings, to ensure that the display combines a modern look with approachability. 
- Maintain a high-end corporate minimalist design, featuring clear visual hierarchy, full-width sections, and strategic use of white space to enhance clarity.
- Utilize professional and concise icons for practice areas and social media links. Icons should be in the Royal Blue accent color to align with brand identity.
